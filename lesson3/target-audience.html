Site Purpose Target Audience Design

Audience

We are targeting entrepreneurs both new and established in business, and other business representatives.

Personas:

Daniel is a young entrepreneur who is starting an ice cream business. He is looking to join the Chamber of Commerce to promote his business and learn about business from other members.

Age:25
Income: 60,000$
Education: Bachelors in agriculture
Familiarity: Unfamiliar with the Chamber of Commerce
Accesses site: from phone

    Scenarios:

  • Looking up what the Chamber of Commerce does
  • Trying join the Chamber of Commerce
  • Looking for contact information
  • Looking for the Chamber's social media presence
  • Looking into the business help the chamber offers

Velma is the owner of a local boutique and a member of the Chamber of Commerce.

Age:42
Income: 200,000$
Education: Bachelors in fashion design
Familiarity: Very familiar with the Chamber of Commerce
Accesses site: from computer.

    Scenarios:

  • Looking at the up coming events the Chamber is planning
  • Trying to contact the Chamber about concerns
  • Looking into become a board member
  • Reviewing the Chamber's initiatives
  • Updating her account information
