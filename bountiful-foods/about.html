Our Story

In 1978 Lucia S. Bountiful traveled the world looking the most delicious strawberry known to man. She found it in Carlsbad California. After she finished mourning the loss of the most delicious strawberry, she dedicated her life to growing and selling produce that could rival it's flavor.
